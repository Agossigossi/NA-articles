Top 6 Browser Games To Cure Boredom

BY NA ARTICLES



6. Run 3

Run 3 is a classic game to play both at school and at home. You are constantly running forwards, and you must jump and move left and right. Touching a wall can flip the gravity, making this game a difficult game to master!


5. Rocket League

Rocket League is an unblocked game you can play at school. Use arrow keys to move your car and push the ball in the opposing players' net! While there is a real version of this game, this school version is just as fun!


4. Steal a brainrot

Play Steal a Brainrot at school for free! Find different brainrot characters walking on a pathway, and buy them so they join your collection. Attempt to steal from other players, but don't let them steal yours!


3. Among Us

Play Among Us online! Complete tasks, figure out who the impostor is, and try not to get eliminated. It's a fun multiplayer game to play with friends or classmates!


2. Baseball Bros

Looking for a great baseball game to play?Baseball Bros is the perfect game for any baseball fan!


1. Golf hit game

Play Golf Hit and challenge your friends! Hit the ball through crazy courses and try to reach the hole in as few strokes as possible.
